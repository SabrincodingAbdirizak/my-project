About us

Founded in Somalia in 2018 and granted a license by the Central Bank of Somalia in the same year,
Cautious Bank is a privately held, Sharia-compliant commercial bank. A new Somalia holds promise,
and we believe in it. The reconstruction of Somalia involves all of us. We can change things if we work
together. This ideology serves as the foundation for our efforts to support the community and eonomic
evelopment of Somalia and to reconstruct the country. Our primary range of financial services includes
retail banking, business and corporate banking, mobile and internet banking, ATM banking, SWIFT transfers,
international money transfers, and much more.
